About DRF NYC

Dorm Room Fund NYC is a student-run venture firm that invests in student-run companies in the Greater New York area. Our team is all students and our aim is to help our peers build amazing companies. We do this by providing initial seed financing (generally $20,000), business and legal resources, and connections to experienced mentors and the broader DRF student community. Above that, as a team, we're all passionate about helping our peers start amazing companies, and are here to help our fellow students in any way we can (even if we aren't an investor). Learn more about our initial work.

Have a Startup?

We aim to be the first choice for student entrepreneurs in the greater New York region who need capital to take their businesses forward. We'd love to hear more about what you're working on. Tell us about your company for possible Dorm Room Fund funding and partnership.
