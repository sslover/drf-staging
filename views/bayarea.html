About DRF Bay Area

Dorm Room Fund SF is a student-run venture firm that invests in student-run companies in the Greater Bay Area. We are here to help our peers pursue their ideas, launch new ventures and build amazing companies. Along the way, we provide seed funding (generally $20,000), business and legal resources and connections to a fantastic team of mentors who have built their own successful businesses. We are by students, for students.

Have a Startup?

We aim to be the first choice for student entrepreneurs in the greater Bay Area region who need capital to take their businesses forward. We'd love to hear more about what you're working on. Tell us about your company for possible Dorm Room Fund funding and partnership.
