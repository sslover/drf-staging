About DRF Philly

Dorm Room Fund is a student-run venture firm that invests in student-run companies. Our team is all students and our mission is to inspire and support more careers in the startup industry. We believe that a relatively small amount of capital can help student founders take their ideas from the dorm room to the market.

The Dorm Room Fund aims to be the first choice for student entrepreneurs in the greater Philadelphia region who need capital to start their amazing businesses.
